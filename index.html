About

I am a researcher primarily interested in seeking human health.

Our experimental approach is interdisciplinary: we combine physiological, pharmacological, psychophysical, and bioinformatics approaches to understand the function of retinal circuits in resting conditions. We use molecular techniques to genetically target specific molecules in the retinal network. We juggle between experimental models and human studies, depending on the questions we have to solve. In particular, we collaborate with local clinicians at IMO, with optometrists in Mexico and Canada, vision scientists, and psychiatrists in France.
